Rules & Terms

Sports Betting Rules

All sports bets are settled in accordance with our general betting rules. Match results are based on the official result at the end of regulation time unless otherwise stated.

  • In-play bets are settled on the final score at the end of the event.
  • Abandoned or postponed events may be voided and stakes returned.
  • Odds are subject to change until the bet is confirmed.

Casino & Games

Casino games and card games follow their respective game rules. Random Number Generators (RNG) are used where applicable to ensure fair play.

  • Minimum and maximum stakes are displayed per game.
  • Disputes must be raised within 7 days of the transaction.

Account & Payments

You must be 18+ and in a jurisdiction where betting is legal. One account per person. Funds must be used for betting only; withdrawal terms apply.

  • Minimum deposit and withdrawal amounts may apply.
  • KYC verification may be required for withdrawals.

Responsible Gambling

We support responsible gambling. Set deposit limits, take breaks, and seek help if betting affects your life. Self-exclusion options are available in your account.
